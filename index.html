OUR DNA

meetings re:imagined is a venue that aims to deliver productive, collaborative and innovative meetings. We do this by providing fun and creative spaces for you and your teams to be yourselves, to think clearly and to work together to create solutions that traditional spaces do not facilitate.

We offer start to end packages and a range of spaces, all designed to foster this creativity in different ways. Talk to us about your needs, and our in-house team will guide you to a solution that suits you.

THE DIFFERENCE

meetings re:imagined aims to do things differently. We provide everything that a more traditional venue would, and build on this with our own unique touches. All our rooms are unique in style but can be adapted in a way that the physical setup matches the workflow you need to make your day productive.

Our state of the art audio-visual systems and mood lighting is fully controllable from a tablet and our on-site team are all specialists, tasked only with making your day productive.

TRANSPORT

meetings re:imagined is located near Southampton, Hampshire just 3 minutes’ walk from a main-line station with direct and regular trains to London, Birmingham and Manchester. Southampton Airport is just 5 minutes’ walk away.Road access is excellent, with the M27 and M3 motorways both less than 5 minute’s drive.

As well as being on the doorstep of Southampton, we are also close to Portsmouth and Bournemouth. Beyond transport, there are many local hotel options if needed, and we will happily advise on options.
